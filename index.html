Research Areas

Quantum & Semiclassical Gravity

Planck-scale quantum effects and their imprint on classically curved space-time.

Main Goal: Understand how quantum fluctuations affect geometry across scales.

Ongoing Work: Covariant Wick rotation, singularity avoidance, GUP implications.

Selected Output: Relativistic GUP (Phys. Rev. D 105, 2022)

Black Hole Physics

Interior structure, horizon thermodynamics and information retention.

Main Goal: Model black-hole interiors beyond the singularity.

Ongoing Work: Two-phase quasiparticle core model, entropy bounds.

Selected Output: Black‑Hole Interior Modelling (arXiv:2506.02944)

Quantum Geometry

Covariant uncertainty principles and Wick-rotated metric phases.

Main Goal: Develop covariant formulations of quantum spacetime structures.

Ongoing Work: Euclidean caps and entropy, quantum reference frames.

Selected Output: Covariant Wick Rotation (EPJC 83, 2023)

Early‑Universe Cosmology

Geometric mechanisms capable of seeding, driving and ending cosmic inflation.

Main Goal: Reconstruct inflationary dynamics using geometric tools.

Ongoing Work: Euclidean bridges, entropy-based inflation exit conditions.

Selected Output: Gravitational Decoherence in Curved Space-Time (arXiv:2302.09038)
