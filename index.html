Core Research Questions & Long‑Term Goals

  • Can space‑time singularities be removed within a covariant semiclassical framework?
  • What are the truly relativistic limits of quantum uncertainty?
  • How does Wick rotation encode thermodynamics and quantum phases of gravity?
  • Does curvature itself decohere quantum matter?

Vision ⟶ A covariant, experimentally anchored picture of quantum space‑time where horizons, curvature and quantum information are treated on equal footing — linking black‑hole interiors, Planck‑scale physics and the dynamics of the early universe.
