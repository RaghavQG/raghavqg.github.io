About Me

Postdoctoral Fellow at Ariel University working on the interface between gravity and quantum theory.

  • Core Questions: Planck-scale microstructure of spacetime; semiclassical hints toward quantum gravity.
  • Approach: Covariant Wick rotations, entropy corrections by extrinsic curvature, Riemann-bound decoherence.
  • Values: Open collaboration, clear communication, and playful exploration.

Research Areas

Quantum & Semiclassical Gravity

Planck-scale quantum effects and their imprint on classically curved spacetime.

Main Goal: Covariant frameworks for spacetime breakdown.

Ongoing: Singularity resolution via Wick rotations.

Output: Covariant Wick Rotation

Black Hole Physics

Interior structure, horizon thermodynamics, and information retention.

Main Goal: Thermodynamic signatures of nonsingular cores.

Ongoing: Quasiparticle-core models; entropy matching.

Output: Black-Hole Interior Modelling

Quantum Geometry

Covariant uncertainty principles; Wick-rotated metric phases.

Main Goal: Lorentz invariance vs. quantum limits.

Ongoing: Tensor GUP; path integrals.

Output: Relativistic GUP

Early-Universe Cosmology

Geometric mechanisms for inflation: seeding, driving, exiting.

Main Goal: Covariant inflation from Euclidean bridges.

Ongoing: Geometric inflation; graceful exits.

Output: Cosmic Decoherence Study
