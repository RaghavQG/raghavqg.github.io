About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at Ariel University, Israel, working with Prof. Sergey Bondarenko. My research is broadly focused on the interplay between gravity and quantum theory, exploring foundational aspects of spacetime.

I deeply enjoy collaborative research, engaging discussions, and presenting findings at academic venues worldwide.

Research Interests

  • Semiclassical and Quantum aspects of Gravity
  • Foundational aspects of spacetime structure
  • Quantum field theory in curved spacetime
